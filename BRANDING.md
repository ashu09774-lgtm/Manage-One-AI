# TaskFlow AI Branding

## Product Name

TaskFlow AI

## Logo Direction

- Primary mark: rounded square with lightning glyph
- Product wordmark: `TaskFlow AI`
- Tone: fast, reliable, operational, intelligent

## Color Direction

- Primary: energetic blue-based accent already used in the app UI
- Surface: neutral dashboard-first dark/light surfaces
- Supporting colors: green for completion, yellow for review, red for alerts

## Product Positioning

TaskFlow AI is an AI-powered workspace for planning, execution, collaboration, and automation.

## Visual Principles

- Productivity-first layout
- Clear hierarchy
- SaaS-style dashboard polish
- Minimal friction in forms and task flows
